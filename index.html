How does it work:

The WIWL app gives you the current weather and the temperature (you can easily toggle between Celsius and Fahrenheit with the switch at the bottom of the app), humidity and wind values and also provides a 4-days weather forecast for the desired location.

The app will ask your permission to use your current location.
If you deny the permission, or if your device does not support the Geolocation API, you will be able to search the desired place name.
To obtain updated weather info, or to search the weather for another place, you have to click on the refresh icon at the bottom of the window.
The app's icons are based on the 'Simple Weather Icons' library by Daniel Vierich
The background image is courtesy of Picture author

Weather data source: OpenWeatherMap

I hope you will enjoy it!

Can we localize you?

The WIWL app can use the Geolocation API to get your current position with only two more click of your mouse.

If you click on the 'OK' button, your browser will ask you if you would like to share your location with the app; you have to allow it.

If you click on the 'Nope' button or if you close this message you will be brought back to the app and you will need to type the desired location name in the search box and click on the compass to get the local weather.

In both cases you have just to wait few moments to get your weather info.
